A collection about storytelling must tell its own story. With my focus placed firmly on the interpersonal interactions of humans I knew I wanted the story to be about perspective. I eventually landed on this as my starting statements to then later expand upon for each garment:

Humans group humans. Here they are grouped by their worldly priorities.

Me: The individual is focused on themselves, propelled through life focused and driven by the ideals of their own success and that alone.









You: Love for the fellow man, the success of others is what matters. Offering help at every turn is the driver of happiness and life love.









Us: Connected with those similar - fearful of outsiders and differences. Invested in horrors of ignorance, isolation, conservatism, and ostracism based on the properties of man.









Everyone: We are one, we are a collective, peace, prosperity and populous perseverance are possible for our people, all people.
