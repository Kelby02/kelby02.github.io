This is the creative journey I went on in the creation of my graduate collection. Four complete looks over ~250 days, guided only by the below brief and my own thoughts.
The Brief:
Immerse yourself in the education virtualisation of the fashion industry studio & workroom by integrating the essential skills of research, design, innovation, pattern making and construction.

Formulate a unique and original design concept that reflects your own design aesthetic by conducting extensive primary and secondary research.

Breaking boundaries is essential - think differently, exercise freedom of expression : on the journey to produce an aesthetically pleasing, original, innovative, cohesive, sophisticated, and well-constructed complete runway collection.

Setting my starting point at 'Storytelling', I began The Journey.
